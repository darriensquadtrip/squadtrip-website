## The differences between WeTravel and SquadTrip

Like SquadTrip, WeTravel is a web-based booking management solution and payment gateway specifically designed for group travel organizers, travel agents, and travel businesses that organize multi-day and group travel. But there are notable differences between SquadTrip and WeTravel that are worth calling out. Let’s take a look.

### WeTravel Pros & Cons

#### Pros:

-   **Booking Software:** WeTravel helps travel companies create booking pages and manage online bookings.
-   **Payment Processing:** WeTravel lets approved users process credit card payments, offer payment plans to customers, and offer instant transfers to suppliers worldwide.
-   **Streamlined Operations:** WeTravel automates travel management tasks like invoicing and communication, allowing travel agency businesses and tour operators to save time and reduce errors.

#### Cons

-   **Acceptance is NOT Guaranteed:** Not everyone can use WeTravel. If you only receive partial verification, any funds collected through WeTravel won’t be paid out until 30 days after your trip has ended.
-   **No Auto-Billing for Free Accounts:** WeTravel’s auto-billing feature is not available unless you pay for the “Pro” or “Enterprise” plans. This means you won’t be able to create payment plans that automatically charge your customers on predefined dates.

### SquadTrip Pros & Cons

#### Pros:

-   **No Complicated Approval Process:** You can sign up with SquadTrip and start using our all-in-one group travel software and booking engine for free today!
-   **Unmatched Customer Support:** SquadTrip works closely with trip organizers to offer support whenever you need it. We’re constantly releasing [new features and updates](https://squadtrip.com/product-updates/) based on [feedback from our customers](https://squadtrip.featurebase.app/). This isn’t just about providing a powerful booking platform or tool, we want you to succeed at building a business that transforms your life.
-   **Connect With More Travelers:** You can list your group trip on SquadTrip’s Group Trip Marketplace to increase bookings and connect with travelers outside of your network.

#### Cons

-   **Some payment methods aren’t accepted:** Travelers can pay in full or make payments toward their trip via credit card. ​​We recently activated Klarna, Afterpay, and Apple Pay for all organizers so that you and your travelers can enjoy the added flexibility and convenience of these popular payment options. However, SquadTrip does not currently accept payments from travelers via PayPal, Venmo, or ACH.

## How much does WeTravel cost?

WeTravel’s pricing model starts with a free “Basic” account that has fewer features, limited customization, and no customer auto-billing.

Its paid plans start at $79/month for the “Pro” plan, but the “Enterprise” plan requires contacting their team for a custom quote. Paid plans let you unlock custom branding for trip pages, more fund payout and transfer options, and API integrations that you can’t get on the “Basic” plan.

## How much does SquadTrip cost?

SquadTrip pricing starts with its free “Starter” plan, which is great for individuals planning a group trip, bachelor party, or family reunion. The Starter plan gives you access to all of SquadTrip’s advanced features you need to manage your group trip, including our advanced Trip Builder, Reporting Dashboard, Automatic Payment Plans, and Email Messaging.

SquadTrip’s paid plans are ideal for group trip enthusiasts who want to start a travel business and professional brands, businesses, and travel agents who want to grow their businesses and offer additional services to group travelers. Pricing starts at $29/month for the “Launch” plan, and $99/month for the “Premium” Plan, with discounts available when you pay annually.

SquadTrip’s processing fees are 6%. These fees include Stripe’s merchant charge of 2.9% plus an additional 30 cents for handling credit card payments.

## Who is WeTravel best for?

WeTravel caters to approved travel businesses that organize multi-day group trips, often for corporate events, retreats, or educational tours.
