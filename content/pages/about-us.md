## About SquadTrip

SquadTrip is a SaaS platform designed to empower small travel and tour operators by providing an all-in-one solution for creating trips, collecting payments, managing bookings, and marketing their trips.

We understand the unique challenges faced by group travel organizers, and our mission is to make group travel easier and more enjoyable for everyone involved.

Founded by Darrien Watson and Stevon Judd, avid travelers and passionate entrepreneurs, SquadTrip aims to streamline the process of planning and executing group trips. Whether you’re organizing a birthday trip, a destination wedding, a family reunion, or any other group adventure, our platform is built to save you time and reduce the hassle of collecting payments and managing reservations.

![](https://squadtrip.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/Frame-427319832-1.png) ![Clients](https://squadtrip.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/Frame-427319832-2.png)

At SquadTrip, we believe in the power of travel to bring people together. Our team is dedicated to supporting our users every step of the way, from onboarding and setup to strategic advice and ongoing support.

We emphasize a personal touch, ensuring that our team is always accessible to our users, and we provide tools like text message blasts and email marketing to help you stay connected with your travelers. Join SquadTrip today and discover how we can help you grow your travel business and make every trip a success.

## Our mission

Empower anyone to create amazing travel experiences.
