## [Installment Reschedule](#installment-reschedule)

Organizers can now choose to allow travelers to take control of their auto payment schedule. If enabled, travelers can effortlessly reschedule their payment dates to match their schedule and financial preferences. They can select any date between the day after the current date and the last day to book the trip.

To enable installment rescheduling, go to:

Trip Management → Trip Builder → Payments → Select Package → Toggle “Allow Installment Reschedule”

Repeat for each package you want to enable installment rescheduling for. _Don’t forget to save your changes!_

[See the travelers’ view](https://squadtrip.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/installment-reschedule_traveler-walkthrough.gif)

![](https://squadtrip.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/installment-reschedule_walkthrough2.gif) Enhancement

## [Retry Failed Payments](#retry-failed-payments)

If an auto-charged payment fails, the system will schedule another attempt to collect the payment in 2 days. On the day of the failed payment, the traveler will see the failed payment status. The day after the failed payment, the system will change the label and the date to the next scheduled date. Both the Organizer and the traveler will be notified via email.

[Learn more about traveler payments](https://intercom.help/squadtrip/en/articles/9804529-making-payments-on-your-account)

## [Processing Fee Handling](#processing-Fee-Handling)

By default, Payment Processing Fees are paid by your travelers. However, when you create a trip package type, you can choose who will pay the Payment Processing Fees.

-   **Absorb fees:** Click All fees are paid by organizer if you want the fees to be included in the price you set. Fees will be deducted from your sales.
-   **Pass fees on:** Click All fees are paid by traveler if you want attendees to pay the fees on top of the price you set. Fees will be collected off the top of your sales.

Trip organizers have the power to absorb the processing fee, ensuring travelers enjoy a seamless booking process without encountering any unexpected processing fees at checkout. Plus, we’ve made pricing a breeze by introducing a handy calculator, so you can effortlessly determine the total cost of your packages and add-ons, with fee charges included.

[View complete guide on processing fee options](https://intercom.help/squadtrip/en/articles/9789927-processing-fee-handling)
