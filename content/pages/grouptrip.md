## Collect Money for Your Alumni Group Trip

Want to organize and collect money for your alumni group trip?

Offering payment plans are essential if you want to collect money for your trip. With SquadTrip you can build a landing page where guests can make a deposit and enroll in a monthly payment plan with auto-billing. No more sending receipts, invoices, or reminders, SquadTrip does all the hard work once they enroll in a payment plan.

### HOW IT WORKS
