### Step 1

## Define Your Niche

As a beginner, you need to specialize in your niche. A good start is to define your role and what value you bring to the potential clients. Many agencies with no prior experience often jump to creating trips without defining their focused niche, this is a big red flag.

If you’re unsure, begin by listing out your goals and aspirations, and what role you want that represents and defines your travel agency among others.

**For reference, travel agents mainly help people plan trips by offering:**

-   Booking assistance for flights, hotels, cruises, and tours.
-   Expert advice on travel destinations, itineraries, vacation packages, and pricing.
-   Personalized travel planning for individuals, families, and groups.
-   Other important travel arrangements.

**Many agents also specialize in certain types of travel, such as:**

-   Group travel
-   Wellness retreats
-   Luxury travel
-   Cruise lines
-   Wedding destinations
-   Adventure trips
-   Corporate retreats

This allows them to focus on creating unique, targeted travel experiences to meet specific client needs. You can choose to focus on one area at the start, build a strong reputation, and then slowly expand to other areas.
