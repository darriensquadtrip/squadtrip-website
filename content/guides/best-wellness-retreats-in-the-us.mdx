## 6 Best Wellness Retreats in the US 2026 : Top Places to Reset

Below are the top picks for 2026. Each retreat offers something different so you can choose what fits your travel style.

### 1\. Miraval Arizona Resort and Spa, Tucson

Miraval is one of the most famous wellness retreats in the country. Travelers choose it for its desert scenery, award winning spa, and wide mix of classes.

You get meditation sessions, [fitness activities](/guides/fitness-retreats-training-recovery-guide) , equine experiences, nutrition coaching, and creative workshops. The environment is quiet and calm. Phones stay on airplane mode in many areas, which helps you disconnect.

Food is fresh, organic, and portion balanced. The staff focuses on mindful eating, movement, and stress reduction.

Best for travelers who want a full service retreat with guided experiences every day.

### 2\. Canyon Ranch, Lenox, Massachusetts

Canyon Ranch offers deep wellness programs with expert led sessions. Travelers often book their stay to focus on healthy routines, fitness, and long term lifestyle change.

You get personalized plans that include exercise, nutrition, sleep coaching, and spa therapies. The property is surrounded by nature and feels peaceful in every season.

Guests like the flexibility of choosing structured programs or creating their own schedule. There are plenty of activities such as yoga, hiking, cooking lessons, and restorative treatments.

Best for travelers who want a more educational approach to wellness.

### 3\. The Art of Living Retreat Center, North Carolina

This retreat center is known for meditation, breathwork, and yoga programs. Visitors appreciate the quiet setting in the Blue Ridge Mountains.

Programs range from weekend stress relief stays to long self exploration retreats. Ayurvedic treatments are popular here and are handled by trained practitioners.

Food is vegetarian and prepared with simple, clean ingredients. The center has a calm energy that helps guests slow down and reset their mind.

Best for travelers seeking emotional balance and deep inner relaxation.

### 4\. Ojai Valley Inn, California

Ojai Valley Inn feels luxurious without losing the relaxed retreat vibe. The location is surrounded by mountains and has warm California weather most of the year.

The spa village is a major highlight. Travelers enjoy the outdoor pools, massage therapies, and mindfulness classes. You can also join art workshops, cycling tours, and nature walks.

Ojai is known for its sunsets, local organic food, and peaceful atmosphere. It works well for couples, solo travelers, and small groups.

Best for travelers who want a mix of wellness and luxury.

### 5\. Sedona Mago Center for Well Being and Retreat, Arizona

Sedona’s natural red rock surroundings make this retreat feel magical. The center focuses on healing practices, energy balancing, meditation, and Tai Chi.

Programs help guests release mental tension and reconnect with their body. You get guided sessions, outdoor hotspots, fire ceremonies, and spiritual workshops.

The environment is extremely peaceful. Many travelers say they feel grounded and clear after spending a few days here.

Best for travelers who want a spiritual style wellness experience.

### 6\. The Ranch Malibu, California

The Ranch Malibu offers results driven programs built around fitness, clean eating, and structured routines. It is known for its challenging but rewarding schedule.

Each day includes hiking, strength training, yoga, recovery sessions, and organic meals. Guests come here to reset or jump start their fitness journey in a supportive environment.

The property feels secluded and quiet. Staff members guide you through every part of the program so you always know what to expect.

Best for travelers who want a transformational retreat focused on physical change.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1\. How do I know which wellness retreat is right for me in 2026?

Most travelers choose based on their goal. If you want meditation and breathwork, places like the Art of Living Retreat Center work well. If you want fitness and structure, The Ranch Malibu is a better fit. If you want spa time and luxury, Ojai Valley Inn is a good pick. The content on this page breaks down what each retreat does best so you can match it with your travel style.

### 2\. What makes these six retreats stand out from others in the US?

They all have strong programs, experienced instructors, peaceful settings, and a clear structure that helps travelers reset. Each one offers something different, from heavy fitness to spiritual healing to luxury wellness. They also consistently rank high for guest experience and long term results.

### 3\. Are these retreats good for beginners or people who have never done a wellness trip before?

Yes, most of them work well for beginners. Miraval Arizona, Canyon Ranch, and Ojai Valley Inn offer flexible schedules, so you can ease into activities. The Ranch Malibu is the only place that may feel intense if you prefer a slow start, since the program is more structured and fitness focused.

### 4\. How far in advance should I book a wellness retreat for 2026?

For popular retreats like Miraval, Canyon Ranch, and The Ranch Malibu, many travelers book three to six months ahead. Spring and fall fill up the fastest. Booking early also helps you secure better room choices and program dates.

### 5\. What type of results should I expect from a wellness retreat?

Most travelers leave with better routines, clearer thinking, improved sleep, and a stronger connection to their body. Fitness based retreats may show quicker physical results. Meditation or spiritual retreats help more with emotional clarity and stress reduction.
