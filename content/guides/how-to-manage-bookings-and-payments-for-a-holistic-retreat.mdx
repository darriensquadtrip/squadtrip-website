## FAQs: Managing Bookings and Payments for a Holistic Retreat

### What’s the best way to manage bookings for a holistic retreat without using spreadsheets?

The best way is to use a centralized booking platform that combines registration, payments, and guest information in one place. Spreadsheets may work for very small groups, but as your holistic retreat grows, tracking deposits, installment plans, and room types manually becomes overwhelming and increases the risk of mistakes.

### How far in advance should I start collecting payments for a holistic retreat?

Most holistic retreat hosts begin collecting deposits 3 to 6 months in advance. This gives guests enough time to plan and allows you to secure vendors and venues confidently. Offering installment plans during this period can significantly increase early sign-ups.

### Should I require a non-refundable deposit for my holistic retreat?

Yes, many retreat organizers require a non-refundable deposit to secure a guest’s spot. This protects your revenue and reduces last-minute cancellations. Make sure your cancellation and refund policy is clearly stated on your booking page to avoid confusion.

### How do I manage room assignments for shared and private options?

The easiest way is to create separate pricing tiers for shared and private rooms directly within your booking system. This allows you to set capacity limits for each category and prevents overbooking a specific room type for your holistic retreat.

### What’s the safest way to collect payments for a holistic retreat?

Use a secure online payment processor that encrypts payment data and protects guest information. Avoid collecting sensitive financial details manually via email or messaging apps. A professional booking system builds trust and reduces liability.
