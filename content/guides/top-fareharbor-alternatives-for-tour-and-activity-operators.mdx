### 2\. Rezdy

Rezdy is one of the most advanced booking systems for tour and activity operators. It supports complex scheduling, real time inventory, and reseller connections. Operators who work with distributors or large channels often choose Rezdy because of its strong marketplace.

**Key Features**

Website booking engine  
Channel distribution tools

### 3\. Bókun

Bókun is owned by Tripadvisor, which gives it a strong advantage for distribution and visibility. It is widely used by activities and attractions that sell through multiple channels and need flexible pricing tools.

**Key Features**

Booking widget for websites  
Channel manager  
Inventory management  
Custom pricing and add ons  
Multi location setup

**Best For**

Operators who want strong Tripadvisor and OTA integrations.

**What to Consider**

The dashboard can feel busy if you only need basic features.

### 4\. Xola

**Key Features**

Online and phone bookings  
Guest profiles and CRM  
Conversion tracking  
Abandoned booking recovery  
Flexible scheduling tools

Operators who want marketing support and deeper insights into their booking funnel.

### 7\. Bookeo

**Key Features**

-   Flat pricing
-   Add ons and pricing rules
-   Email alerts
-   Easy setup

**Best For**

Operators who want a low cost booking solution.

**What to Consider**

### 8\. Regiondo

**Key Features**

-   Real time availability

European operators and businesses serving international travelers.

**What to Consider**

### 9\. TripWorks

TripWorks focuses on speed and simplicity. It provides a modern interface with straightforward scheduling and payment tools, making it easy for teams to use.

**Key Features**

-   Fast setup
-   Clean scheduling tools
-   Simple payment processing
-   Availability control
-   Team permissions

**Best For**

Operators who want a modern and easy booking system.

**What to Consider**

### 10\. WaveRez

WaveRez is designed for water based activities such as boat rentals, jet ski rentals, water sports, and beach experiences. Its resource scheduling is built for vessels, equipment, and time slots.

**Key Features**

-   Real time rental availability
-   Resource and vessel management
-   Flexible payment settings
-   Website booking widget
-   Custom booking rules

**Best For**

Operators in the marine and water sports industry.

**What to Consider**

Not ideal for multi day tours or land based activities.

## How to Choose the Right FareHarbor Alternative

-   Identify your operational needs. Know whether you run tours, rentals, group activities, or multi-location setups.
    
-   Review your full guest journey. The booking flow should be fast, simple, and feel on brand.
    
-   Compare all pricing and fees, including monthly costs, payment fees, and per-booking charges, so you understand your margins.
    
-   Test resource management. Check how each platform handles guides, boats, vehicles, equipment, or anything you schedule daily.
    
-   Review integrations such as Stripe, Google Analytics, Tripadvisor, Viator, or your CRM.
    
-   Choose a system that can support long term growth to avoid another migration later.

## FAQ

### 1\. Why do tour and activity operators look for FareHarbor alternatives?

Most operators switch when they want a simpler setup, lower fees, better branding, or stronger support. Others move because they need features FareHarbor does not prioritize, such as automated payment plans, rental inventory control, or deeper channel distribution.

### 2\. Which tool is best for water based activities and rentals?

WaveRez works well for boat rentals, jet skis, parasailing, and other marine or waterfront activities. It has resource scheduling designed specifically for equipment and vessels.

### 3.  Which alternative is best for tour operators that also rent equipment?

Checkfront is a strong choice because it handles both tours and rentals in the same calendar. You can manage boats, gear, bikes, or vehicles along with guided tours from one system.

### 4\. What is the most budget friendly alternative to FareHarbor?

Bookeo usually offers the most predictable and affordable pricing since many plans do not charge commissions. Operators with smaller booking volumes often save money by switching to it.

### 5\. Which platform has the strongest OTA marketplace connections?

Rezdy and Bókun are the top choices for operators who rely heavily on distribution. They integrate deeply with Tripadvisor, Viator, GetYourGuide, and partner resellers.
