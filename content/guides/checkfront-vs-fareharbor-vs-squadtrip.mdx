## FAQ

### **1\. Why do people say SquadTrip is better for retreats than Checkfront or FareHarbor?**

Most retreat hosts ask this because other platforms feel like they’re built for quick bookings. SquadTrip gives you payment plans, guest dashboards, forms, and document collection, which you actually need when running a multi day trip. Checkfront and FareHarbor don’t offer that kind of ongoing coordination.

### **2\. What kind of businesses still benefit more from Checkfront or FareHarbor?**

If you run tours, time slot activities, or rentals with equipment or guides, Checkfront or FareHarbor is the better match. They’re designed around scheduling and resources, not long form trip coordination.

### **3\. Does SquadTrip help with traveler communication before the trip starts?**

Yes. Forms, reminders, and updates all go through the dashboard. This is one of the biggest differences between SquadTrip and the other platforms, which rely mostly on email.

### **4\. How does each platform handle cancellations or refund rules?**

SquadTrip allows flexible policies tied to payment plans. Checkfront supports detailed rules. FareHarbor lets you set cancellation windows. For retreat style trips with long lead times, SquadTrip’s structure fits better.

### **5\. Do Checkfront or FareHarbor offer automated form reminders?**

Not in the same way. They focus on reservation logistics and confirmations. SquadTrip automates form reminders because retreats need more guest data.
