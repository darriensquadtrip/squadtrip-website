## How to Automate Travel Payment Follow Ups the Right Way

Automation isn’t just about sending reminders. It’s about creating a complete payment structure that runs in the background.

Here’s how to do it properly.

### Step 1: Use Structured Installment Plans

Most late payments happen because there’s no structure.

**Instead of saying:**  
“Pay the remaining balance by July.”

**Create:**

-   Deposit at booking  
    
-   Installment 1 on fixed date  
    
-   Installment 2 on fixed date  
    
-   Final balance deadline  
    

When payments are broken into smaller, scheduled installments, travelers are more consistent.

With SquadTrip, you can create flexible installment plans directly on your trip page.

### Step 2: Set Automatic Pre-Due Reminders

The best follow up is the one sent before a payment becomes late.

**Good automation includes:**

-   Reminder 7 days before due date  
    
-   Reminder 3 days before due date  
    
-   Reminder on due date  
    

This dramatically reduces overdue payments.

Instead of reacting to missed payments, you prevent them.

### Step 3: Trigger Automatic Overdue Notifications

**If a traveler misses a payment, the system should:**

-   Send an automatic overdue email  
    
-   Clearly show remaining balance  
    
-   Include a direct payment link  
    
-   Maintain a professional tone  
    

This removes awkward personal follow ups.

The message becomes system-generated, not emotionally charged.

-   Total trip revenue  
    
-   Amount collected  
    
-   Amount outstanding  
    
-   Individual traveler balances  
    
-   Payment history  
    

Without logging into multiple platforms.
