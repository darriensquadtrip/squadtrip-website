## FAQs: Travel Agency Reservation Systems

### What is a travel agency reservation system?

A travel agency reservation system is software that helps agencies manage bookings for flights, hotels, tours, and activities from one dashboard. It handles availability, pricing, customer details, payments, and confirmations so agents don’t need to rely on spreadsheets or manual follow-ups.

### How is a travel agency reservation system different from an OTA?

Online travel agencies (OTAs) sell travel directly to customers, while a travel agency reservation system is a backend tool used by agencies to manage and sell travel themselves. With a reservation system, agencies keep control over pricing, branding, and customer relationships.

### Do small travel agencies really need a reservation system?

Yes. Even small agencies benefit from a travel agency reservation system because it reduces manual work, speeds up bookings, and helps avoid errors. Many modern systems are affordable and scale as the agency grows.

### Can a travel agency reservation system handle group bookings?

Most modern systems can manage group bookings, shared itineraries, split payments, and automated reminders. This is especially useful for agencies organizing group trips, retreats, or corporate travel.

### Does a travel agency reservation system support online payments?

Yes. Most platforms support online payments through credit cards, bank transfers, and sometimes installment plans. This allows agencies to collect payments securely and send automatic receipts to clients.
