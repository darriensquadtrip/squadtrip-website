## FAQ

### 1\. If I already use FareHarbor for tours, do I really need a different tool like SquadTrip for retreats?

Many hosts try to reuse FareHarbor for retreats, but the moment you need payment plans, itineraries, room options, or document uploads, the setup becomes messy. That’s usually when hosts realize they need a platform built for group travel instead of time-slot bookings.

### 2\. Why doesn’t SquadTrip focus on OTA integrations like FareHarbor?

Because group trips don’t rely on OTAs the way time-slot tours do. Most retreats and multi day programs are sold through communities, creators, hosts, or private groups, so OTAs aren’t a priority.

### 3\. How do I know if my trip is considered a “group trip” and not just an activity?

If your guests travel together, stay overnight, need forms, or pay in installments, you’re running a group trip or retreat. Tools like FareHarbor and Peek weren’t built for that level of coordination.

### 4\. Is SquadTrip really free to start, or is the free plan limited?

It’s genuinely free to start. You get the core features and can upgrade only if you want more advanced tools or higher limits.

### 5\. Is SquadTrip good for hosts who don’t want a complicated backend?

Yes. The setup is straightforward. Most hosts say they can publish a full booking page with packages, forms, and payment plans in less time than it takes to configure one tour in FareHarbor.
