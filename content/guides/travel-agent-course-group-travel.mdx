## Welcome & How to Use This Kit

For Group Travel Beginners, Career Switchers & Side Hustlers

Welcome! If you’re reading this, you’re probably thinking something like:

-   __“I love planning trips, but I have no idea how to turn it into a real business.”__
-   __“I don’t have a travel background… can I really do this?”__
-   _“I keep seeing people host group trips. How do I start?”_

Good news: you’re in the right place. And yes — you absolutely can.

This kit is built specifically for first-time travel advisors who want to launch a business around group travel — even if you’re starting from scratch. It’s designed to support:

-   Beginners exploring travel as a new income stream
-   Career switchers looking to break into a flexible industry
-   Side hustlers who want to test the waters before going all in

Whether your goal is to plan luxury birthday trips, wellness retreats, reunions, or girls’ getaways — this guide will help you get started the right way.

* * *

## What This Kit Includes

Inside, you’ll find:

-   A step-by-step guide to launching your travel business
-   Real-world tools, checklists, and templates
-   Marketing ideas, pricing strategies, and supplier guidance
-   A focus on group travel (where the best margins and growth potential live)
-   A simple system for organizing payments, forms, and bookings using SquadTrip

This isn’t fluff. It’s the exact roadmap many successful travel advisors wish they had when they started.

* * *

## How to Use This Kit

You can treat this like a course, a playbook, or a checklist. Here’s what we recommend:

1.  Read the chapters in order. Each one builds on the last — from niche selection to pricing your first trip.
2.  Use the Toolkit. Throughout the guide, you’ll find links to templates and tools — Google Docs, spreadsheets, Canva templates, and SquadTrip walkthroughs.
3.  Bookmark your favorites. This kit is meant to be reused. As your business grows, you’ll come back to sections again and again.
4.  Launch with confidence. You don’t need to have everything perfect to take your first client. Follow the roadmap, and improve as you go.
