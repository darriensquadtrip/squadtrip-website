## Feature Overview

### TravelJoy

TravelJoy positions itself as an all in one workspace for travel advisors. It bundles CRM, itineraries, communication, payments and workflow tools into one platform. Advisors who want a simple system to manage every client from start to finish often start here.

**Key features include:**

-   Branded proposals and itineraries  
    
-   Clean client communication workspace  
    
-   Payments and invoicing  
    
-   CRM with task management  
    
-   Templates for faster workflows  
    
-   Trip collaboration for teams  
    

TravelJoy is built for advisors who work with individual clients or small groups and want one system to keep everything organised.

### Travefy

Travefy is one of the most popular tools among travel agents and agencies. Its biggest strength is its itinerary builder. Many advisors choose it because it makes trip presentations look polished and professional.

**Key features include:**

-   Drag and drop itinerary builder  
    
-   Proposal and quote creation  
    
-   CRM and client database  
    
-   Client portal and mobile viewing  
    
-   Website and landing page builder  
    
-   Supplier libraries and easy imports  
    

It suits agencies that produce detailed itineraries, handle multi city trips and want strong client facing visuals.

### **SquadTrip**

[SquadTrip](https://squadtrip.com/) focuses on group trip bookings. It is best for retreats, destination events, hosted group tours and experiences where many guests need to register and pay in instalments.

**Key features include:**

-   Branded trip pages for group travel  
    
-   [Payment plans with automated billing](https://help.squadtrip.com/en/articles/9790124-setting-up-payment-plans-with-auto-billing)  
    
-   Deposit and instalment tracking  
    
-   Guest registration and questionnaires  
    
-   Add ons and upgrade options  
    
-   Central dashboard for all guests  
    

SquadTrip is built for group travel organisers who care more about payments, guest management and registration than traditional itinerary building.

## Use Cases and Ideal Fits

**TravelJoy** is ideal for advisors who want one tool for CRM, payments and itinerary templates. It is simple, clean and practical for one to one clients.

**Travefy** is ideal for travel advisors and agencies that care about beautiful itineraries and client presentations. It is strong for multi city trips and agency scale workflows.

**SquadTrip** is ideal for group trip leaders. If you organise retreats, fitness groups, girls trips, destination weddings or corporate events, this tool handles payments and guests better than traditional itinerary builders.

## Detailed Comparison

### Itinerary Building and Client Experience

-   **TravelJoy** offers branded itineraries and proposals but focuses more on workflows.  
    
-   **Travefy** is the strongest in this category. Its builder is visual, flexible and easy to share.  
    
-   **SquadTrip** focuses on trip pages and booking flows rather than multi day layouts.  
    

**CRM and Client Data**

-   **TravelJoy** has a practical built in CRM with tasks and pipelines.  
    
-   **Travefy** includes a CRM but it may not suit advisors who need advanced data management.  
    
-   **SquadTrip** stores guest information and forms but is not a full CRM.  
    

### Payments, Invoicing and Financial Workflows

-   **TravelJoy** supports payments and invoicing inside the platform.  
    
-   **Travefy** supports proposals, invoices and commission tracking.  
    
-   **SquadTrip** leads in payment flexibility with instalments, auto billing and group tracking.  
    

### Branding and Client Presentation

-   **TravelJoy** offers branded proposals and email templates.  
    
-   **Travefy** provides white label itineraries, landing pages and client facing mobile layouts.  
    
-   **SquadTrip** allows branded trip pages that look modern and help guests book quickly.  
    

### Scalability and Team Collaboration

-   **TravelJoy** works well for solo advisors and small teams.  
    
-   **Travefy** is built for agencies and teams needing advanced trip layouts.  
    
-   **SquadTrip** scales easily for large group trips with many travellers.  
    

**Pricing Considerations**

Each platform uses different pricing styles. TravelJoy offers monthly plans. Travefy offers subscription based pricing that scales with usage. SquadTrip offers a free plan with booking fees plus paid monthly subscriptions. Your choice should match your workflow volume.

## FAQ

### **1\. Which platform is best if I want clients to see everything on a clean mobile interface?**

Travefy is known for polished, mobile-friendly itineraries. SquadTrip’s guest dashboard is also simple to use for payments and trip details. TravelJoy is functional but not as visually focused as Travefy.

### **2\. How different are the onboarding experiences between the three platforms?**

TravelJoy is quick to set up because everything lives in one workspace. Travefy requires some time if you want full itinerary customisation. SquadTrip setup is simple, especially if you’re creating group trips with payment plans.

### **3\. Can I manage a mix of private trips and group trips on the same system?**

You can use any of them, but the best fit depends on what you do more often.

-   Private clients: TravelJoy or Travefy.
    
-   Group trips: SquadTrip.
    

### **4\. How does each platform handle refund requests for travellers?**

Refund workflows depend on the payment provider. SquadTrip uses Stripe’s refund process with clear tracking. TravelJoy and Travefy handle refunds through their connected payment processors.
