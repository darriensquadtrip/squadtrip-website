## What to Look for in a WeTravel Alternative

Before we dive into the ranking, let’s quickly cover what matters when comparing travel tech platforms. WeTravel alternatives should be evaluated on the following:

### Core Features That Matter

-   **Booking management:** Ability to create trip pages and handle reservations  
    
-   **Payments and payouts:** Flexibility in [payment plans](https://squadtrip.com/guides/payment-plans-for-trips/), split payments, and payout timing  
    
-   **Guest communications:** Automated messaging and reminders  
    
-   **Customization:** Branding, forms, and [itinerary](https://squadtrip.com/guides/group-trip-itinerary-planning-step-by-step-guide/) tools  
    
-   **Scalability:** Support for both small groups and larger volume sales  
    

Different tools excel in different areas, so the “best” platform depends on your needs.

## 2\. TravelJoy – Best for Solo Agents & Freelancers

TravelJoy is a platform designed for travel advisors, small agencies, and freelancers who want a streamlined booking and payment experience.

### Key Features

-   Professional booking pages  
    
-   Client communication tools  
    
-   Integrated payments (including split payments)  
    

TravelJoy is often recommended for independent agents because of its polished client interface and easy proposals. While it might not support large group features out of the box like SquadTrip, it offers strong payment tools and client management suited to smaller operations.

## 3\. Travefy – Best for Itinerary Planning

Travefy is known for its itinerary creation and client collaboration features. It’s often favored by travel planners who prioritize detailed trip itineraries over integrated payment flows.

### Strengths

-   Comprehensive itinerary builder  
    
-   Client collaboration dashboards  
    
-   Integrations with calendars and documents  
    

If your priority is crafting customized itineraries and sharing them with clients, Travefy shines. However, it doesn’t always include the same level of payment automation as WeTravel or SquadTrip, so it’s best paired with separate payment tools.

## 4\. Rezdy – Best for Activity & Tour Operators

Rezdy is a specialist booking platform focused on tours, activities, and experiences. It offers powerful booking engines and distribution tools that help operators sell spots efficiently.

### What Rezdy Offers

-   Activity and tour scheduling  
    
-   Multi-channel distribution  
    
-   Integrated payment processing  
    

Rezdy is highly rated among tour operators and local experience providers. While it’s not always positioned as a direct rival to WeTravel for full trip planning, it’s an excellent choice if your core business centers on activities and excursions.

## 5\. Peek Pro – Best for Tour Operators with High Support Needs

Peek Pro is another compelling alternative if tours and scheduled experiences are your bread and butter. It provides booking software designed for tour operators and local activities providers, with strong support and reporting features.

### Highlights of Peek Pro

-   Professional booking engine  
    
-   Guest messaging and upsell tools  
    
-   Detailed reporting  
    

Peek Pro matches well with businesses that need robust support and analytics paired with bookings and payments, especially at scale.

## 6\. FareHarbor – Best for Local Tours & Rentals

FareHarbor is a popular choice for local tour operators and rental businesses. It offers a customizable booking experience with online reservations, payment support, and management tools.

### What Makes FareHarbor Good

-   Custom booking pages  
    
-   Payment processing  
    
-   Inventory and schedule management  
    

This makes FareHarbor a solid solution for operators focused on local offerings rather than multi-day group travel experiences.
