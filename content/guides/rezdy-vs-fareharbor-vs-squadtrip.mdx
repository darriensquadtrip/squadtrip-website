## Understanding Rezdy vs FareHarbor vs SquadTrip

Here is a quick overview so you can see how each platform positions itself in the market.

### – SquadTrip

SquadTrip is built specifically for group travel. It gives hosts a clean way to present their trip, offer automated payment plans, collect detailed forms, and manage guests through an intuitive dashboard. For retreats, workshops, coaching trips, destination programs, and community travel, SquadTrip fits naturally into the workflow from signup to arrival.

### – Rezdy

Rezdy focuses on tours and activities that have real time availability. It connects operators to online travel marketplaces such as Viator and GetYourGuide. It works best if your business sells multiple activities per day and depends on live scheduling.

FareHarbor is designed for tour and rental companies that need staff management, calendars, POS systems, and operational controls. It works well for water sports, charters, zip lines, and day activities.

## FAQ

### **1\. What makes SquadTrip’s payment plans better than Rezdy or FareHarbor?**

SquadTrip lets you set automated installments, deadlines, reminders, and deposit options. Rezdy only supports basic deposits. FareHarbor allows partial payments but not long term plans. This is why retreat hosts prefer SquadTrip for higher ticket trips.
