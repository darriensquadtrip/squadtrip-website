## Collin Devon Events

[@collindevonevents](https://www.instagram.com/collindevonevents/) | [cdeantigua.com](https://cdeantigua.azurewebsites.net/)

A TIME Magazine feature later, what was intended to be a one time “Graduation Getaway” has grown into one of most critically acclaimed group trips for diverse millennial travelers. Today, it’s said the only way to experience Antigua & Barbuda’s exotic cuisine, beautiful beaches, and thrilling nightlife is with Collin Devon Events (CDE).

* * *

## Curated by Khlo

[@curatedbykhlo](https://www.instagram.com/curatedbykhlo/) | [curatedbykhlo.com](https://curatedbykhlo.com/)

Khloe Summer is a blossoming travel vlogger/content creator whose passion for travel has led her to teach and inspire other women around the world to step out of their comfort zones and travel. She gives women the confidence to live their “best lives” and create the lives of their dreams, with no hesitation. Through this mindset she has garnered a loyal and growing fanbase. At CuratedByKhlo.com, this thriving fanbase can find community, content, travel consultations and, most importantly, the best itineraries to cities and countries all over the world, personally Curated By Khlo.

## Winners Only Weekend

[@winnersonlyweekend](https://www.instagram.com/winnersonlyweekend/) | [winnersonlyweekend.com](https://www.winnersonlyweekend.com/)

Traveling with the Winners allows you the ultimate opportunity to travel like a local by embedding yourself in the culture of a destination. We will expose you to the greatest aspects your visiting destination has to offer. Be ready to try some of the most unique foods, drinks and practices the world has to offer.

## Passport Society

[@passport.society](https://www.instagram.com/passport.society/) | [passportsocietytravel.com](https://www.passportsocietytravel.com/)

Passport Society is a collective of travelers who venture beyond domestic borders and comfort zones to seek a world of adventure with like-minded, fun, mature, and energetic travelers; looking to create new memories through curated experiences.

* * *

## En Root Travel

[@enroottravel](https://www.instagram.com/enroottravel/) | [enroottravel.com](https://www.enroottravel.com/)

En Root Travel was created to curate high quality, all inclusive trips that connect HBCU alumni to Black cultures throughout the African Diaspora.

* * *

## JetQuest Travel

[@jetquest](https://www.instagram.com/jetquest/) | [jetquest.net](https://www.jetquest.net/)

JetQuest is a travel company that provides travelers with an all-encompassing travel experience. They specialize in creating a stress-free adventure that includes accommodations, airport transfer, culture, security & camera crew, excursions, tours, and nightlife activities. No matter if your alone or with a group of friends, everyone is invited to join their special trips where you can make lasting friends and even network with others around the world.

* * *

## Acrxss Africa

[@acrxssafrica](https://www.instagram.com/acrxssafrica/)

For the last 5 years, Acrxss Africa has taken over 200+ people to Africa where they experienced returning home in a special way. Enjoy meeting, mixing, and mingling with a diverse group of travelers as you experience all that Africa has to offer.

* * *

## Black Travel Vibes

[@blacktravelvibes](https://www.instagram.com/blacktravelvibes/) | [blacktravelvibes.com](https://www.blacktravelvibes.com/)

Black Travel Vibes is a Black Owned Travel Company curating exhilarating group trips. Their group trips are packed with excursions and adventurous experiences. They take pride in bringing our Melanin Kings and Queens across the globe, having fun and immersing ourselves within the culture.

* * *

## Queer Queens Travel

[@queerqueenstravel](https://www.instagram.com/queerqueenstravel/) | [queerqueenstravel.com](https://www.queerqueenstravel.com/)

Queer Queens Travel is a travel collective for Queer Women of Colour by Queer Women of Colour. They plan and execute group trips that highlight community, charity and cultural immersion and showcase QWOC who travel and inspire others to face their fears and explore the world.

* * *

## B.A.G. Travel

[@bagtravelgroup](https://www.instagram.com/bagtravelgroup/) | [bagtravel.net](https://www.bagtravel.net/)

B.A.G. Travel is an immersive adventure travel consultant for social groups and organizations. They also provide visa/passport services, travel insurance protection, and tickets to the hottest concert, sports, and theatre events worldwide!

## BME Kween

[@bmekween](https://www.instagram.com/bmekween/) | [bmekween.com](https://bmekween.com/)

Dr. N’Dea Irvin-Choy is a biomedical engineer turned travel influencer. In addition to her work as a travel content creator, influencer, and model, Dr. N’Dea hosts group trips specifically catered to solo travelers.
