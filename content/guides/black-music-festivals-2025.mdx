## Afrofuture Fest

##### December 2025 –January 2026  
Accra, Ghana

#####   
[afrofuture.com](https://www.afrofuture.com/)

Afrofuture Fest (formerly Afrochella) mixes rich African heritage with electrifying entertainment filled with art installations, live music, and a journey through Ghana’s profound historical sites. With likely musical performances by top African artists such as Burna Boy, Wizkid, Davido, Stonebwoy, and Kwame Eugene, alongside other big names in the music scene, this festival is a vibrant celebration where you and your crew can connect, dance, and soak in the transformative spirit of Africa.

The Crop Over summer festival is Barbados’ most popular and colorful festival. It features events such as the Opening Gala, Bridgetown Market, Cohobblopot, Kiddies Kadooment, and the Grand Kadooment parade. The festival celebrates Barbadian culture with calypso music, arts and crafts, and elaborate costumes.
