**TL;DR :**

- **SquadTrip** is built for group trip organizers, retreat hosts, and travel businesses that need booking pages, payment collection, and guest management in one platform
- **Travefy** is built for travel advisors who need polished itineraries, client proposals, and CRM features
- SquadTrip offers a **free plan** with transaction-based pricing; Travefy starts at **$49/month per user** with no free tier
- For **payment collection**, SquadTrip is the clear winner with payment plans, automated reminders, and real-time tracking
- For **itinerary presentation**, Travefy has stronger visual tools and template libraries
- Many travel professionals **use both platforms together** — Travefy for proposals and SquadTrip for bookings and payments
- The right choice depends on whether your business centers on **selling and managing group experiences** (SquadTrip) or **advising individual clients with custom itineraries** (Travefy)

## Introduction

Choosing the right platform for your travel business is one of the highest-impact decisions you will make. The wrong tool creates extra work, frustrates your clients, and costs you money. The right tool automates the tedious stuff and lets you focus on growing your business.

SquadTrip and Travefy are two of the most popular platforms in the travel industry, but they are built for **fundamentally different use cases**. Comparing them without understanding those differences leads to the wrong choice.

This guide breaks down both platforms honestly — features, pricing, strengths, and weaknesses — so you can make an informed decision based on how your business actually works. If you have already seen our [three-way comparison with TravelJoy](/guides/traveljoy-vs-travefy-vs-squadtrip-the-best-group-travel-management-platforms-compared), this guide goes deeper on the SquadTrip vs. Travefy matchup specifically.

## SquadTrip Overview

**SquadTrip** is a group travel management platform designed for organizers who sell and manage group experiences — retreats, group trips, destination weddings, church trips, team travel, and more.

The platform is built around the idea that **group travel is fundamentally a booking and payment management problem**. When you are coordinating 10, 20, or 50 travelers, you need to know who has booked, who has paid, who still owes money, and what information you have collected from each person. SquadTrip centralizes all of that.

### Core Features

- **Booking pages** — branded, shareable pages where travelers can view trip details, select options, and book with payment
- **Payment collection** — accept credit cards and other payment methods directly through the platform
- **Payment plans** — split trip costs into installments with automated collection and reminders
- **Guest management** — track every traveler's booking status, payment history, and personal information
- **Traveler information collection** — custom forms to gather passport details, dietary needs, emergency contacts, and more
- **Communication tools** — message travelers through the platform
- **Trip dashboard** — real-time overview of bookings, revenue, and outstanding balances

### Who Uses SquadTrip

- Retreat hosts and wellness coaches
- Travel agents and agencies specializing in group travel
- Group trip organizers and influencers
- Destination wedding planners
- Church and organization trip coordinators
- Tour operators running group experiences

### Pricing Model

SquadTrip offers a **free plan** that lets you create booking pages and collect payments with a per-transaction fee. Paid plans unlock additional features at competitive monthly rates. This model means you can start using the platform without any upfront cost and only pay more as your business grows.

## Travefy Overview

**Travefy** is an itinerary and proposal platform designed primarily for travel advisors who create custom travel plans for individual clients and small groups.

The platform is built around the idea that **travel advising is fundamentally a presentation and client management problem**. When you are pitching a custom trip to a client, you need beautiful proposals, detailed itineraries, and a system to track your client relationships. Travefy centralizes that workflow.

### Core Features

- **Itinerary builder** — drag-and-drop tool with a large library of pre-built content, maps, and images
- **Client proposals** — visually polished trip proposals you can send to clients for approval
- **CRM** — client relationship management with contact records, trip history, and notes
- **Invoicing** — generate and send invoices to clients
- **Supplier management** — organize supplier contacts and booking information
- **Trip templates** — reusable itinerary templates for common destinations and trip types
- **Brand customization** — add your logo, colors, and branding to itineraries and proposals

### Who Uses Travefy

- Independent travel advisors and consultants
- Travel agencies focused on custom luxury travel
- Destination specialists
- Honeymoon planners
- Corporate travel planners

### Pricing Model

Travefy charges a **monthly subscription per user**, starting at $49/month for the Professional plan and going up to $99/month for the Professional Plus plan. There is no free tier. Annual billing offers a discount, but you are still committing to a recurring cost regardless of how many trips you book.

## Feature-by-Feature Comparison

### Booking Pages

**SquadTrip:** Full booking page functionality with trip details, pricing tiers, add-on options, and integrated payment processing. Travelers can browse the trip, select their options, and complete payment in one flow. Booking pages are shareable via link and can be embedded on your website.

**Travefy:** Does not offer dedicated booking pages. Clients receive proposals and itineraries via email or link, but there is no self-service booking flow with integrated payment. The advisor handles bookings manually after the client approves a proposal.

**Verdict:** SquadTrip wins clearly here. If your business involves travelers booking and paying online — especially for group trips — SquadTrip's booking pages are a core feature that Travefy simply does not match.

### Payment Collection

**SquadTrip:** Built-in payment processing with support for credit cards and multiple payment methods. Key differentiators include:

- **Payment plans** with automated installment collection
- **Automated payment reminders** sent to travelers before due dates
- **Real-time payment tracking** showing who has paid, who owes, and total revenue
- **Partial payments and deposits** with balance tracking
- **Group payment dashboards** for managing multiple travelers' payments at once

**Travefy:** Offers basic **invoicing** — you can create and send invoices to clients, and some plans support payment links. However, Travefy's payment features are designed for traditional advisor-client billing (one invoice to one client) rather than group payment management.

Travefy does not offer:

- Automated payment plans with installment scheduling
- Automated payment reminders
- Group payment tracking dashboards
- Per-traveler payment status in a group context

**Verdict:** SquadTrip is significantly stronger for payment collection, especially for group travel. If you manage trips where multiple travelers need to make multiple payments on different schedules, SquadTrip's payment infrastructure saves hours of manual work every week. For solo advisory work where you send one invoice to one client, Travefy's invoicing may be sufficient.

### Itinerary Tools

**SquadTrip:** Provides trip detail sections on booking pages where you can outline the itinerary, inclusions, and schedule. Functional and informative, but not designed as a standalone itinerary presentation tool.

**Travefy:** This is Travefy's flagship feature. The itinerary builder includes:

- **Drag-and-drop editor** with pre-built content blocks
- **Integrated maps** showing destinations and routes
- **Photo libraries** with destination imagery
- **Day-by-day formatting** with times, descriptions, and notes
- **Supplier details** embedded in each itinerary item
- **Mobile-optimized** viewing for travelers
- **Template library** for reusable itinerary structures

**Verdict:** Travefy wins on itinerary presentation. If your business depends on wowing clients with beautiful, detailed trip proposals, Travefy's itinerary builder is a best-in-class tool. SquadTrip provides the information travelers need, but the presentation is functional rather than aspirational.

### Group Management

**SquadTrip:** Purpose-built for groups with features including:

- **Per-traveler profiles** with booking status, payment history, and collected information
- **Group dashboards** showing all travelers, their statuses, and outstanding actions
- **Bulk communication** to all travelers in a trip
- **Custom information collection** per traveler (passport, dietary needs, medical info)
- **Room assignment and add-on tracking** per person

**Travefy:** Manages groups through its CRM and itinerary tools. You can create group itineraries and track group members as contacts, but the platform does not offer the same depth of per-traveler management for bookings and payments. Group features are available but are an extension of the individual client model rather than a core design principle.

**Verdict:** SquadTrip is the stronger platform for group management. Managing 20 travelers with different payment statuses, room assignments, and dietary restrictions is exactly what SquadTrip was built to do. Travefy can handle groups but requires more manual work to track the operational details.

### Communication

**SquadTrip:** In-platform messaging to individual travelers or entire groups. Payment reminders and booking confirmations are automated. Communication is tied to the booking context, so messages reference specific trips, payments, and details.

**Travefy:** Client communication through the platform with proposal sharing and itinerary delivery. Notes and interactions are logged in the CRM. Communication is tied to the advisory relationship rather than specific bookings.

**Verdict:** Different strengths. SquadTrip's communication is booking-focused and operational (payment reminders, logistics). Travefy's communication is relationship-focused and advisory (proposals, itinerary updates). Choose based on your primary communication needs.

### Pricing Comparison

| Feature | SquadTrip | Travefy |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| Free plan | Yes | No |
| Starting price | Free (transaction fees apply) | $49/month per user |
| Payment plan cost | Included | Not available |
| Annual commitment required | No | Discount available for annual |
| Per-user pricing | No | Yes |

**Verdict:** SquadTrip has a lower barrier to entry with its free plan and transaction-based model. You pay as you earn. Travefy requires a monthly commitment whether you book one trip or fifty. For new organizers, side-hustle coaches, and small businesses, SquadTrip's pricing model is significantly more accessible. For established agencies with consistent volume, Travefy's flat monthly rate may be predictable and manageable.

## Who Should Use SquadTrip

SquadTrip is the right platform if:

- You **organize group trips** of any kind — retreats, vacations, destination events, church trips, team travel
- **Payment collection** is a major part of your workflow and you need to track multiple travelers' payments
- You want to **offer payment plans** to make higher-ticket experiences accessible
- You need **booking pages** where travelers can self-register and pay without manual back-and-forth
- You are a **coach, creator, or organizer** who sells group experiences directly to consumers
- You want to **start free** and scale your costs with your revenue
- You value **operational efficiency** — knowing at a glance who has booked, who has paid, and what information is missing

SquadTrip excels when the core challenge is **managing the logistics of getting a group of people booked, paid, and organized**. The platform was designed from the ground up for this exact problem.

## Who Should Use Travefy

Travefy is the right platform if:

- You are a **travel advisor** who creates custom itineraries for individual clients or small groups
- Your business depends on **beautiful, detailed proposals** that win clients
- You need a **CRM** to manage long-term client relationships and trip history
- Your clients expect **polished visual itineraries** with maps, images, and day-by-day detail
- You **do not need to collect payments** through the platform (or basic invoicing is sufficient)
- You work primarily with **individual travelers or couples** rather than large groups
- You have a **consistent monthly income** that supports subscription pricing

Travefy excels when the core challenge is **presenting and selling custom travel experiences** to clients who expect a high-touch, visually impressive advisory experience.

## Can You Use Both?

**Yes, and many travel professionals do.** Using SquadTrip and Travefy together gives you the best of both worlds:

1. **Use Travefy to win the client.** Create a stunning itinerary proposal that showcases your expertise and the trip details. Send it to your client for approval.

2. **Use SquadTrip to manage the booking.** Once the client says yes, set up a SquadTrip booking page with pricing, payment plans, and traveler information collection. Share the booking link with all travelers in the group.

3. **Use SquadTrip for ongoing itinerary updates.** As you finalize supplier bookings and trip details, update the itinerary in SquadTrip so travelers can view it in their account and download it as a PDF.

4. **Use SquadTrip for payment tracking and group management.** Monitor who has paid, send reminders, collect outstanding balances, and manage traveler logistics through SquadTrip's dashboard.

This workflow is especially common among **travel advisors who specialize in group trips** — they need Travefy's presentation layer for the sales process and SquadTrip's operational layer for the management process.

The key is understanding which tool handles which part of your workflow. When each platform is used for its strengths, they complement rather than compete.

## The Verdict

SquadTrip and Travefy are both excellent platforms, but they solve **different problems for different types of travel businesses**.

**Choose SquadTrip if your business is built around group experiences.** If you spend your days managing group bookings, chasing payments, and coordinating multiple travelers, SquadTrip will save you hours every week and help you collect more revenue. The free plan means you can try it without risk, and the payment plan features alone may justify the switch.

**Choose Travefy if your business is built around custom travel advisory.** If you spend your days crafting bespoke itineraries for individual clients, presenting proposals, and managing long-term client relationships, Travefy's itinerary builder and CRM will elevate your professional image and streamline your advisory workflow.

**Choose both if you do a mix of both.** There is no rule that says you can only use one platform. The most effective travel professionals match their tools to their tasks.
