## Top 10 Eventbrite Alternatives for Retreats and Group Trips

### 1\. SquadTrip

SquadTrip is the best Eventbrite alternative for retreats and group travel because it is built specifically for multi day experiences, payment schedules, guest management, and organized communication. Eventbrite focuses on ticketing, while SquadTrip gives you everything needed to run an actual trip without relying on spreadsheets or multiple apps.

The booking experience is clean and easy to follow. You can create a branded page, upload your itinerary, [set payment plans](/guidesoffer-payment-plans-to-customers), and automate reminders. Guests get their own dashboard where they can view trip details, download receipts, and update information. This reduces manual follow ups for organizers.

The platform works well for retreats, team travel, wellness programs, student trips, international group travel, and destination based events. Because everything is automated, it saves time and reduces errors.

**Key features:**

-   Automated payment plans
-   Guest dashboard
-   Simple booking pages
-   Full itinerary builder
-   Flexible pricing options
-   Auto reminders for due payments
-   Stripe integration
-   Clean organizer dashboard

SquadTrip is ideal if your event requires structured bookings instead of simple ticket sales.

### 2\. WeTravel

WeTravel is popular among retreat leaders, nonprofit groups, yoga instructors, and travel organizers. It offers strong payment processing features, allowing you to collect deposits, partial payments, and international cards. The booking pages look professional and have enough customization to present a premium experience.

The platform also provides money movement tools like payouts, vendor payments, and financial tracking. These help retreat owners run operations without switching tools. Eventbrite cannot handle travel payments or multi part payment structures, which is where WeTravel stands out.

**Best for:**  Retreat creators, yoga groups, fitness coaches, travel companies

**Why choose it:**  Strong payment workflow, good platform for professional retreat businesses

**Read More:**  [Best WeTravel Alternatives](/guidesbest-wetravel-alternatives)

### 3\. Travefy

Travefy is built for itinerary creation and client communication. It is not a direct Eventbrite competitor in ticketing, but it is often used by retreat organizers who want to create visually appealing itineraries for guests before or after they book.

Its drag and drop builder helps you design trip plans that feel personalized. You can send them to guests as shareable links or PDFs. It also supports messaging, forms, and branded proposals. If your trip involves detailed day by day plans, Travefy can help you present them clearly.

**Best for:**  Itinerary creators, travel planners, retreat hosts who want visual trip plans

**Why choose it:** Beautiful itinerary builder, easy collaboration, clean mobile experience

**Read More:** [Travefy Alternatives](/guidestravefy-alternatives-for-travel-agents-and-group-trip-planners)

### 4\. Regpack

Regpack focuses on registration and enrollment workflows. It works well for camps, group programs, workshops, and community events. Many retreat owners choose Regpack because it lets you create conditional forms, onboarding questions, discount logic, and custom workflows for different types of guests.

Unlike Eventbrite, it allows flexible payment schedules and automated billing. The admin side is strong for segmentation, analytics, and registrant tracking. If your event requires collecting detailed guest information, this is a strong pick.

**Best for:** Camps, educational retreats, leadership programs

**Why choose it:** Form customization, flexible billing, advanced registration tracking

### 5\. Cvent

Cvent is a major enterprise event management platform. It fits large retreats, conferences, corporate team outings, and high budget experiences. The platform includes registration, ticketing, hotel room block management, check in tools, and reporting.

If you run corporate retreats or large scale travel events where logistics are complex, Cvent gives you control. It is more powerful than Eventbrite but also more expensive. For smaller trips, it may feel too heavy.

**Best for:** Corporate retreats, professional events, large travel programs

**Why choose it:** Advanced event tech, strong reporting, enterprise level support

### 6\. Ticket Tailor

Ticket Tailor is a simple and affordable alternative to Eventbrite with low fees. It works best for smaller events, gatherings, and one day experiences. Although it is not tailor made for retreats, many small group hosts use it for simple ticket collection.

The platform is easy to set up and does not charge per ticket service fees like Eventbrite. It is a good fit if your retreat does not require payment plans or advanced travel features.

**Best for:** Small group events, local gatherings

**Why choose it:** Simple and low cost ticketing

### 7\. Explara

Explara is a flexible event management tool with registration, payments, email marketing, and membership management. It offers more workflow features than Eventbrite, especially for communities or recurring programs.

Retreat organizers use Explara when they want a combined system for managing events, attendees, communication, and payments. It also supports multiple currencies and regional payment gateways.

**Best for:** Community retreats, recurring events, workshops

**Why choose it:** All in one platform with email automation and payment support

### 8\. FareHarbor

FareHarbor serves tour and activity operators. It works well for group excursions, adventure trips, city tours, and activity based retreats. It includes scheduling, availability tracking, resource management, and integrations with marketplaces like Viator.

If your retreat includes booked activities such as boat trips, hikes, classes, or tours, FareHarbor helps with operational control. Eventbrite cannot support availability based scheduling, which is why many hosts switch to FareHarbor.

**Best for:** Tours, activities, adventure based retreats

**Why choose it:** Real time availability, resource management, strong marketplace network

**Read More:** [Top 10 FareHarbor Alternatives](/guidestop-fareharbor-alternatives-for-tour-and-activity-operators)

### 9\. Bókun

Bókun is owned by Tripadvisor and is designed for travel suppliers. It fits retreats that involve booked activities, package add ons, or upsells. You can bundle experiences, sell gift cards, offer real time availability, and connect to travel marketplaces.

It also supports dynamic pricing and commission control, which is useful for retreat organizers working with multiple local partners.

**Best for:** Retreats with tours and activities, destination management

**Why choose it:** Marketplace connectivity, package creation, add ons

### 10\. Eventzilla

Eventzilla is a practical choice for workshops, retreats, conferences, and hybrid events. It offers registration pages, scheduling tools, virtual event support, and payment processing.

If you want something more flexible than Eventbrite with a clean interface and customizable registration process, Eventzilla is dependable. It also supports check in tools, branded emails, and attendee tracking.

**Best for**: Retreat organizers who want simple registration with flexibility

**Why choose it**: Custom registration forms, virtual event tools, clean design

## How to Choose the Right Eventbrite Alternative

Choosing the right platform depends on the type of retreat or group trip you are running. Here is a clear process to help you decide.

### 1\. Identify your event type

– Are you hosting a wellness retreat, student trip, corporate retreat, adventure tour, or weekend gathering?  
– The more detailed the trip, the more structure your platform needs.

### 2\. Check payment needs

– Do you need deposits, payment plans, split payments, reminders, or international payment options?  
– Eventbrite cannot support these features well, but most alternatives on this list can.

### 3\. Think about communication

– Guests need updates, receipts, reminders, and itinerary access.  
– Choose a tool that keeps everything in one place.

### 4\. Check if itineraries or activities require scheduling

– Tools like SquadTrip, WeTravel, FareHarbor, and Travefy are designed for trip workflows.

### 5\. Look for automation

– Automation saves hours of manual admin work.  
– SquadTrip automates payment reminders.  
– WeTravel automates financial workflows.  
– FareHarbor automates availability management.

### 6\. Consider the guest experience

– The best platform makes it easy for guests to book, pay, and follow the plan without confusion.

### 7\. Match the tool with your long term needs

– Choose a tool you can continue to use as your retreat business grows.
