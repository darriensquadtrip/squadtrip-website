## 1\. Pacific Coast Highway (California)

### Route: San Francisco to San Diego

The Pacific Coast Highway, also known as Highway 1, is often considered one of the best road trip routes in the USA for a reason.

**Highlights:**

-   Big Sur cliffs  
    
-   Bixby Bridge  
    
-   Santa Barbara  
    
-   Malibu beaches  
    
-   Monterey and Carmel  
    

This route offers dramatic coastal views, winding cliffside roads, and charming beach towns. It’s ideal for couples, [creative retreats](https://squadtrip.com/guides/planning-a-creative-retreat-a-complete-guide-for-hosts-and-coaches/), and friends’ getaways.

**Best Time to Go:**

Late spring through early fall for clear skies and coastal visibility.

For group trips along this route, booking beach houses or boutique hotels in advance is essential. Use SquadTrip to collect deposits early so accommodations are secured without awkward reminders.

## 2\. Route 66 (Illinois to California)

### Route: Chicago to Santa Monica

Route 66 is pure Americana. It stretches across eight states and delivers roadside diners, vintage motels, neon signs, and historic charm.

**Highlights:**

-   Cadillac Ranch (Texas)  
    
-   Grand Canyon detour  
    
-   Historic motels in Arizona  
    
-   Santa Monica Pier  
    

This is a longer road trip and works well for extended vacations or multi-week adventures.

**Best For:**

-   Nostalgia lovers  
    
-   Road trip purists  
    
-   Cross-country bucket lists  
    

Because this is a longer drive, splitting costs across multiple travelers is common. Setting up installment payments in advance can reduce last-minute dropouts.

-   Mountain cabins  
    

The Blue Ridge Parkway is especially stunning in autumn. It’s perfect for wellness retreats, couples trips, or quiet nature-focused escapes.

## 5\. Going-to-the-Sun Road (Montana)

### Route: Glacier National Park

This is one of the most breathtaking mountain drives in North America.

**Highlights:**

-   Alpine lakes

-   Snow-capped peaks

It’s seasonal and usually open from late June through early fall.

Ideal for outdoor-focused groups who want hiking, photography, and scenic stops.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the most scenic road trip route in the USA?

The Pacific Coast Highway in California is often considered the most scenic road trip route in the USA thanks to its dramatic ocean cliffs, coastal towns, and iconic stops like Big Sur. However, routes like the Blue Ridge Parkway and Going-to-the-Sun Road also rank highly for mountain views.

### How many days do you need for a USA road trip?

It depends on the route. Short routes like the Texas Hill Country can be done in 2–3 days, while longer drives like Route 66 or the Alaska Highway may require one to three weeks. For national park loops like Utah’s Mighty 5, 5–7 days is ideal.

### What is the best time of year for a road trip in the USA?

Spring and fall are generally the best seasons due to mild weather and fewer crowds. Fall is perfect for Northeast foliage routes, summer works well for mountain drives like Going-to-the-Sun Road, and winter is ideal for warmer routes like the Florida Keys.

### How do you budget for a group road trip?

Start by estimating gas, lodging, food, park fees, and activities. Add a small emergency buffer. Dividing costs evenly among travelers helps keep things simple. Using a centralized trip page makes collecting payments and tracking expenses much easier.

### Are national park road trips worth it?

Yes. Routes like the Utah Mighty 5 and Glacier National Park offer landscapes you simply cannot experience any other way. They are perfect for hiking groups, photographers, and outdoor-focused trips.
