As more and more of travelers in the Black Travel community spread our wings, we’re noticing a growing trend: solo travel is on the rise! Surprisingly, nearly half of Black travelers are hitting the road solo, mainly because they can’t find anyone to join them.

But Squadtrip is here to save the day for those craving connection on their adventures, we have the perfect solution! Our **Trip Marketplace** is a game-changer, letting Black travelers link up with fellow adventurers on group trips tailored just for us. To give you a taste of the coolest trips out there, we asked some of our favorite Black travel experts for their top picks. Here’s what they had to share.

## DeAnna Taylor

DeAnna Taylor is a criminal defense Attorney turned travel writer, author, and senior-level editor. The Charlotte, NC native has touched all 7 continents, traveled to over 40 countries, and even lived and worked in South Korea. Her passion lies in educating others on how to see the world. Her IG is [@brokeandabroadlife](https://www.instagram.com/brokeandabroadlife/).

## Tomiko Harvey

With a career spanning over 25 years in marketing and a profound impact on the tourism industry for over 9 years, Tomiko Harvey stands as a trailblazer in creating spaces where every traveler’s story is not just heard but celebrated.

As a co-founder of the Black Travel Alliance, Tomiko has been instrumental in reshaping the narrative of travel, ensuring that the rich diversity of experiences within the black community is not only acknowledged but prominently featured. Through innovative campaigns and strategic initiatives, Tomiko has championed the cause of inclusive tourism, fostering an environment where every traveler, regardless of background, feels seen, valued, and welcomed.

Currently also serving as the Director of TBEX North America, Tomiko plays a pivotal role in shaping the discourse around travel blogging and content creation. By fostering collaboration, providing a platform for diverse voices, and staying attuned to the ever-evolving landscape of travel trends, Tomiko continues to steer TBEX toward new horizons.

## Brian Oliver

Brian is a well respected travel blogger, influencer and public speaker. He has served as a keynote speaker at many events both locally and nationwide encouraging others to pursue their passion and goals.

Brian’s love for travel and empowerment began as a young boy. Many days were spent following behind and riding in the car with his grandfather on various local and domestic journeys, as he carried out his duties as a minister.

While many may use travel as an escape from the realities of their cities, he has found it to be a source of inspiration, motivation and encouragement. Brian knows that the privilege of travel is one that must not be taken for granted, but most importantly shared with others.

## Rick Southers

Rick Southers is a lifestyle photographer and filmmaker with an emphasis on highlighting the vibrance of the African Diaspora and their experiences. He calls Pittsburgh home but eagerly travels wherever a story needs to be told.

Rick’s unique perspective showcases the black experience near and far through vibrant color theory and eye-catching composition. His captivating travel photography has landed his work in Travel & Leisure, Essence, and Travel Noire, as well as working with brands such as Ritz Carlton, Nike Swim, the Mexican Tourism Board, and more.
